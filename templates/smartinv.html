SmartInv Workflows

End to End user journey

flowchart TD A[Open SmartInv and sign in] --> B{Your role} B -->|Admin| C[See Admin dashboard] B -->|HR| D[See HR dashboard] B -->|Site| E[See Site dashboard] B -->|Procurement| F[See Procurement dashboard] B -->|Finance| G[See Finance dashboard] B -->|Super Admin| H[See Super Admin dashboard] C --> I[Upload an invoice] D --> I E --> I F --> I H --> I I --> J[System reads invoice details] J --> K[You review and fix details if needed] K --> L{Ready to send for review} L -- Yes --> M[Submit for Finance review] L -- No --> K M --> N[Finance checks invoice] N --> O{Decision} O -- Approve --> P[Status becomes Approved] O -- Reject --> Q[Status becomes Rejected] P --> R[Payment is recorded later] R --> S[Status becomes Paid] Q --> T[You can edit and send again] T --> M
  • Sign in: Enter your email and password.
  • Your home page: You land on a page based on your role.
  • Upload invoice: Add a file. The system reads its details for you.
  • Review details: Check numbers and text. Fix anything that looks off.
  • Send for review: When everything looks right, send it to Finance.
  • Finance review: Finance approves or rejects. If rejected, you can improve and send again.
  • After approval: Once payment is done, the invoice shows as Paid.
